Triton Energy Climate App

By Tyler Lo | Last Updated Feb 16, 2025

Try it Here!

Introduction

Under UCSD Research Professor Mike Ferry, I and 5 other students worked on Triton Energy Climate, an app designed to teach users about renewable energy and what methods are available to us to reach sustainability. It uses 2 million+ data points collected over 30 years of research on climate at UCSD.

The project was coded in React-Native (SORRY, POWELL) with a PostgreSQL backend hosted on DigitalOcean and Render.

UI/UX Design

The UI/UX process was several weeks of back and forth between Mike and the UI/UX team, working on a solution that could encapsulate all of the data that needed to be in an app.

How the app looks

The overall app turned out relatively close to the initial design. 3-4 sprints of redesigns / bugfixes were done to get the app in a more polished state.
